About Connie

Meet the Heart Behind Connie’s Cake Shop

Every cake tells a story, and Connie’s story is one of faith, courage, creativity, and purpose.

After leaving the corporate world, Connie chose to pursue what had always been close to her heart: creating beautiful cakes, delicious pastries, and memorable catering experiences. What began as a passion soon became a calling, driven by the belief that the talents God has given us are meant to serve, inspire, and bring joy to others.

Connie is a creative entrepreneur who believes that life’s most meaningful moments deserve to be celebrated beautifully. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, baby shower, graduation, corporate celebration, or an ordinary day made extraordinary, she pours care, excellence, and attention to detail into every creation.

Known for her vibrant personality, warm heart, and genuine love for people, Connie finds inspiration in everyday beauty. She believes that food has the power to bring people together, and that cakes become part of cherished memories.

At Connie’s Cake Shop, every order is more than just another project. It’s an opportunity to celebrate love, family, milestones, friendship, faith, and life’s beautiful moments.

Her mission is simple:

To create experiences that taste as beautiful as they look and leave lasting memories long after the celebration ends.

When she’s not in the kitchen, you’ll likely find Connie sharing behind-the-scenes moments from her entrepreneurial journey. Through her social media platforms, especially YouTube, she offers an honest look into building a creative business, balancing everyday life, embracing personal growth, and trusting God throughout the journey.

Whether you’re here to order a custom cake, browse our finger foods, or simply gather inspiration, you’re always welcome.

Thank you for stopping by.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you.

With love,
Connie
